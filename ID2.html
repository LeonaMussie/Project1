General Information:

Eritreans holding a National ID do not require a visa to enter Eritrea. (Must be original, copy is not acceptable) A Visa is required for Non-Eritreans and Eritreans without a National ID.

Instructions:

  1. Select passport type and read the corresponding requirement.
  2. Complete and submit the visa application form and fee.
  3. Travel upon receipt of your visa.
Forms:
  • Laissez-Passer Application Form
  • Laissez-Passer Requirement

Laissez-Passer Requirement:

Laissez-Passer
  • Eritrean National ID (for anyone 18 years old or older)
  • Two Photos (35 x 45mm or 1.38′ x 1.77′, White background, facing straight forward, forehead and ears fully visible, prescription glasses only and religious hats or scarfs only are allowed) Permanent Residence Card (copy)
  • Old Eritrean passport (copy)
  • Flight Itinerary
  • Recovery and Rehabilitation Tax Clearance
  • Police Report is required if applying for a Laissez-Passer due to lost Passport
  • Processing Fee – $25 USD Money Order or Cashier’s Check payable to “Embassy of Eritrea” (Non refundable)
  • Mail Services Fee £5
  • Minimum of 10 business days are required for processing.
  • Requirements may change at any time
  • Mail completed form, required documents and payment to: Embassy of Eritrea 1708 New Hampshire Ave NW Washington, DC 20009
