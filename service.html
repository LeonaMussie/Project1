General Information:

Eritreans holding a National ID do not require a visa to enter Eritrea. (Must be original, copy is not acceptable) A Visa is required for Non-Eritreans and Eritreans without a National ID.

Instructions:

  1. Select the visa type and read the corresponding requirement.
  2. Complete and submit the visa application form and fee.
  3. Travel upon receipt of your visa.
Forms: Vissa Form

Visa Requirements:

Business Visa
  • Visa application form B62.3 fully completed and signed
  • Passport size photograph (35 x 45mm or 1.38″ x 1.77″)
  • Proof of Residency or Permanent Resident Card – for non-UK citizens
  • Passport must be valid at least six months beyond the dates of your trip
  • Letter stating purpose and duration of your trip
  • Proof of sufficient funds to cover all expenses
  • Fee:
    • Single $70 GBP
    • 3 Months $100 GBP
    • 6 Months $200 GBP
    • 1 Year $300 GBP
  • Mail Services Fee £5
  • Minimum of 10 business days are required for processing.
  • Requirements may change at any time
  • Mail completed form, required documents and payment to: Embassy of Eritrea 1708 New Hampshire Ave NW Washington, DC 20009
