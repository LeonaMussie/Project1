General Information:

Power of Attorneys can be authenticated by the Embassy of Eritrea and will be used as a legal document to act on your behalf for matters in Eritrea.

Instructions:

  1. Select the Power of Attorney type and read the corresponding requirement.
  2. Complete and submit the Power of Attorney Form application form and fee.
Forms:
  • Power of Attorney Individual Form
  • Power of Attorney Joint Form

POA Requirement:

Individual
  • Current Recovery and Rehabilitation Tax Clearance
  • Completed and signed Power of Attorney form (must be signed in front of Consular Staff or Community Representative)
  • Processing fee £70 money order payable to “Embassy of Eritrea” (Including Mail Services Fee)
  • Content of the Power of Attorney must be written in detail and must be specific.
  • Fees are nonrefundable.
  • Mail completed form, required documents and payment to: Embassy of Eritrea 1708 New Hampshire Ave NW Washington, DC 20009
Joint Group
  • Current RRT clearance for each person individually
  • Completed and signed joint Power of Attorney form (must be signed in front of Consular Staff or Community Representative).
  • Content of the Power of Attorney must be written in detail and must be specific.
  • Processing fee: $70 money order payable to Embassy of Eritrea (Includes Mail Services Fee)
  • Mail completed form, required documents and payment to: Embassy of Eritrea 1708 New Hampshire Ave NW Washington, DC 20009
