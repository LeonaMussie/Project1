General Information:

Passports are issued to Eritrean citizens with National ID and children with Eritrean parents.

Instructions:

  1. Select the passport type and read the corresponding requirement.
  2. Complete and submit the visa application form and fee.
  3. Mail application package to the Embassy.
Forms: Passport Form

Passport Requirements:

New Passport
  • Eritrean National ID (for anyone 18 years old or older)
  • Three Photos
    • Size: 35 x 45mm or 1.38′ x 1.77′
    • White background
    • Centered and facing straight forward
    • Hair off your face so forehead and ears fully visible
    • Natural expression (no smile, laugh or frown)
    • Prescription glasses only
    • Only religious hats or scarfs are allowed
  • Permanent Residence Permit (BRP) (Copy)
  • Recovery and Rehabilitation Tax Clearance
  • Birth certificate (Copy)
  • Processing Fee £200 Money Order or Cashier’s Check payable to “Embassy of Eritrea”
  • Mail Services Fee £5
  • All fees are non-refundable
  • Requirements may change at any time
  • Mail completed form, required documents and payment to: Embassy of Eritrea 1708 New Hampshire Ave NW Washington, DC 20009
Old Passport
  • Eritrean National ID (for anyone 18 years old or older)
  • Three Photos
    • Size: 35 x 45mm or 1.38′ x 1.77′
    • White background
    • Centered and facing straight forward
    • Hair off your face so forehead and ears fully visible
    • Natural expression (no smile, laugh or frown)
    • Prescription glasses only
    • Only religious hats or scarfs are allowed
  • Old Eritrean Passport (Copy)
  • If old passport is lost – police report is required
  • Permanent Residence Permit (BRP) (Copy)
  • Processing Fee £100 for 18 years and older and £75 for Underage Applicants, Money Order or Cashier’s Check payable to “Embassy of Eritrea”
  • Mail Services Fee £5
  • All fees are non-refundable
  • Requirements may change at any time
  • Mail completed form, required documents and payment to: Embassy of Eritrea 1708 New Hampshire Ave NW Washington, DC 20009
